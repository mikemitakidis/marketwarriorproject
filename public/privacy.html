We comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and respect the data privacy rights of all users worldwide.

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you

Contact our Data Protection team at support@marketwarrior.club. We will respond to your request within 30 days. You may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:
